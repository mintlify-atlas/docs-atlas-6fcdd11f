Aster Bot is an automated perpetual futures trading system built for [AsterDEX](https://www.asterdex.com). It connects via WebSocket to the `fstream.asterdex.com` feed, aggregates real-time ticks into synthetic OHLCV bars, and executes trades through two independent strategy engines — **Watermellon** (EMA + RSI trend following) and **Peach Hybrid** (dual V1 bias + V2 momentum surge). Every decision is logged to daily CSV and JSON files for research and compliance review.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to AsterDEX">
    The bot opens a WebSocket connection to `wss://fstream.asterdex.com/ws` and subscribes to the real-time trade stream for your configured `PAIR_SYMBOL`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build virtual bars">
    Raw ticks are accumulated into synthetic OHLCV bars at the interval you set with `VIRTUAL_TIMEFRAME_MS` (default: 30 seconds). This gives strategies a consistent time-series to work with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Evaluate strategy signals">
    On each completed bar, the active strategy engine (Watermellon or Peach Hybrid) computes indicator values and returns a `StrategySignal` — `long`, `short`, or `null`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply risk controls and execute">
    Before any order is placed, the risk layer checks position limits, leverage caps, flip rate, ADX regime filter, and consecutive-loss rules. In `dry-run` mode all execution is simulated. In `live` mode real orders are sent to the exchange.
  </Step>
</Steps>
